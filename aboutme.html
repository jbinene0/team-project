I’m Pierre Lono.

Like many people growing up, I had different dreams about what I wanted to become. My path eventually led me to a career in Occupational Safety and Health, but the journey there was not a straight line.

After finishing high school, I served a two-year full-time mission for my church. When I returned, I began studying biology in college with the goal of attending medical school or pursuing nursing.

After shadowing nurses and surgeons in the operating room, I realized that treating patients was not the path meant for me. Instead of treating injuries after they happen, I wanted to help prevent them in the first place.

After seeking guidance and thoughtful reflection, I discovered Occupational Safety and Health — a field that perfectly aligned with my desire to protect people and promote healthier workplaces.

Welcome to my page and to a career dedicated to protecting people and creating safer environments.
